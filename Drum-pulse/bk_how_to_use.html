Drum Kit

🥁 How to use

  • 🎵 Click any drum button or press its matching key on your keyboard to play the corresponding sound.
  • ✨ Each button will animate when played.
  • Key Mappings:
    w - Tom 1
    a - Tom 2
    s - Tom 3
    d - Tom 4
    f - Snare
    g - Kick Bass
    h - Tambourine
    j - Crash
    k - Open Hi-Hat
    l - Closed Hi-Hat
  • 🎮 Try the “Play Drum Kit Simon” button for a fun memory game!

Have fun creating your own rhythms!

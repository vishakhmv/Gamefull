🥁 Meet Your Drums

  • W - Tom 1
  • A - Tom 2
  • S - Tom 3
  • D - Tom 4
  • F - Snare
  • G - Kick Bass
  • H - Tambourine
  • J - Crash
  • K - Open Hi-hat
  • L - Closed Hi-hat

🎮 How to Play

  • Press the Start button or hit the Spacebar on your keyboard to begin Level 1. The timer will start automatically.
  • Watch carefully — one drum will flash and play a sound. That's your first beat.
  • Press the same drum (or its key on your keyboard) to match the beat and move to the next level.
  • Each new level adds one more drum to the sequence — repeat the full sequence from the beginning at every level.
  • Try to pass as many levels as possible and beat your high score.
  • If you press the wrong drum, the screen will flash red, a “wrong” sound will play, and the game will end. The timer will stop. Press the Start button or Spacebar on your keyboard to restart.

Ready to play? Feel the beat, follow the rhythm, and start drumming!

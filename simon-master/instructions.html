🎮 How to Play

🟢 Step 1: Press any key on your keyboard or the start button to start the game. You will begin at Level 1.

🟡 Step 2: One of the buttons will flash and play a sound. Watch and listen carefully.

🔴 Step 3: Click on the same button that flashed. If you are correct, you go to the next level.

🔵 Step 4: In each new level, the game will add one more color to the sequence. Repeat the entire pattern from the beginning.

❌ If you make a mistake: You will hear a "wrong" sound and the screen will flash red. The game will end, but you can press any key on your keyboard or the start button to restart.

🎯 Goal: Try to remember and repeat the pattern for as many levels as possible to beat the high score!

Caution: The game might prank you, so the general rules may change.
